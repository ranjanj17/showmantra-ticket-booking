# ShowMantra Knowledge Base & FAQ

## Cancellation and Refund Policy
- Tickets can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the showtime.
- A flat cancellation fee of ₹50 per ticket will be deducted from the refund amount.
- Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- Convenience fees and taxes paid during booking are strictly non-refundable.

## Outside Food and Beverages
- For hygiene and security reasons, outside food and beverages are strictly prohibited inside the cinema halls.
- We offer a wide variety of food and beverage options at our concession stands.

## Child Ticket Policy
- Children aged 3 years and above require a full-priced ticket.
- Children below 3 years of age are allowed free of charge provided they do not occupy a separate seat.

## Accessibility
- Wheelchair-accessible seats are available at all our locations.
- Special assistance can be requested at the box office upon arrival.

## Offers and Discounts
- We have a "Tuesday Blockbuster" offer where all standard seats are discounted by 20%.
- Loyalty points (ShowMantra Rewards) can be redeemed during the payment checkout step.
